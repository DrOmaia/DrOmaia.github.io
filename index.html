Research profile

Applied AI, responsibly translated into impact.

My research connects computer vision, healthcare AI, and responsible AI governance with practical challenges in intelligent systems and higher education.

Computer Vision

Object Detection in Real-Time Video Surveillance Using Attention-Based Transformer–YOLOv8

Alexandria Engineering Journal · Elsevier · 2025

The study integrates attention and Transformer components with YOLOv8 for complex real-time scenes, reaching 96.78% precision, 96.89% recall, and 89.67% mAP.

View paper ↗
Healthcare AI

CNN-Based Automated Detection of Metastatic Cancer in Histopathology Images

Engineering, Technology & Applied Science Research · 2025

As first and corresponding author, I presented an EfficientNetB3 and Grad-CAM framework that achieved 92.33% test accuracy and localized malignant regions in over 95% of test samples.

View paper ↗
Responsible AI

Governance and Ethical Frameworks for AI Integration in Higher Education

Journal of Ecohumanism · 2025

As first author, I examined how privacy, algorithmic bias, accountability, capacity building, and legal compliance can guide responsible AI adoption in higher education.
